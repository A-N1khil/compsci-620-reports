\section{Threats to Validity}
This literature survey is subject to several threats to validity. First, the analysis is limited to a selected set of recent studies focused primarily on LLM-assisted programming tools, which may not fully represent the broader landscape of AI-assisted software engineering research. Second, many of the surveyed studies evaluate productivity and interaction behavior using controlled experiments or short-term tasks, which may not accurately reflect long-term industrial development practices. Additionally, differences in experimental design, developer expertise, programming tasks, and evaluation metrics across studies make direct comparison challenging. Finally, the rapid evolution of Generative AI systems means that findings may become outdated as newer models and interaction paradigms continue to emerge.
