\section{Impact of GenAI in the Software Development Process}
The rise of Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) is transforming SDLC activities, particularly requirements engineering. Systems such as ChatGPT can assist in generating, refining, and evaluating requirements with improved consistency and abstraction \cite{aloshan2023zeroshot}. LLMs can also simulate stakeholders in interactive elicitation scenarios, supporting communication and exploratory learning \cite{debnath2020virtualclient}. However, human oversight remains essential to ensure correctness, feasibility, and domain alignment \cite{bang2023multilingualmultimodalevaluation}.

For the purposes of this paper, we focus on four key stages of the SDLC that are particularly impacted by Generative AI: requirements elicitation, coding, debugging, and documentation (Table \ref{tab:stages:re}). These stages represent critical points in the development process where human-AI interaction can significantly influence developer behavior, problem-solving strategies, and overall productivity.
